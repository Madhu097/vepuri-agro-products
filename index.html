About Vepuri

Rooted in Agriculture,
Built for Industry

We are a coconut-based food processing company focused on producing value-added coconut products through structured and hygienic manufacturing practices — transforming mature coconuts into consistent, high-quality food products that meet modern industry requirements.

By combining traditional agricultural understanding with modern manufacturing systems, we contribute to a stronger coconut value chain and support sustainable growth within the food processing sector.
