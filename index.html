About Vepuri

Built on Agriculture
Driven by Value

Vepuri Agro Products Pvt. Ltd. is a coconut-based food processing and value-addition company based in Andhra Pradesh, India. We operate with a clear focus on transforming raw coconut produce into high-value products through structured processing systems, ensuring consistent quality, reliable supply, and better market realization. With direct farmer sourcing, integrated operations, and expanding processing capabilities, Vepuri has built a strong foundation across the coconut value chain connecting agriculture, processing, and markets into a scalable and efficient system.

Our growing product portfolio serves domestic and global requirements, supported by operational discipline, continuous innovation, and a long-term approach to value creation.
